This dataset is for real-world problem, named entity recognition.
We consider two domains, news domain (CoNLL) and email domain (Enron).
For every domain of data, you are provided with Train, Dev and Test.
There are many files in a directory, so you need to combine them together for every set.
The format for data is column. Every raw, you have two parts split by space: word and tag.
Every sentence is split by a space line.
